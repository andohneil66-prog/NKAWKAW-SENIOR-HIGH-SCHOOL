Welcome to NKAWKAW SHS Admissions

Nkawkaw Senior High School is a premier educational institution located in the Eastern Region of Ghana. Established in 1969, we have a tradition of academic excellence and holistic development.

We invite qualified applicants to apply for admission into Secondary Year 1 (BS1) for the upcoming academic year.

Admission Requirements

  • Must have completed BECE (Basic Education Certificate Examination)
  • Minimum aggregate score of 30 or better (depending on track)
  • Age between 12-18 years
  • Good conduct certificate from previous school
  • Birth certificate or Ghana Card
  • Recent passport-size photographs

General Science

  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • Mathematics, Elective Mathematics
  • English, Social Studies

Business

  • Accounting, Economics
  • Business Mathematics, Management
  • English, Social Studies

General Arts

  • Literature, History
  • Government, Philosophy
  • English, Social Studies

Home Economics

  • Biology, Chemistry
  • English, Social Studies

Important Dates

• Application Period: Forms available throughout the year
• Admissions Test: Conducted after form submission
• Release of Admission: Via SMS and school notice board
•Registration: Within one week of admission
